About

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur. I am working under the guidance of Dr. Debapriya Basu Roy. My research area includes Hardware Accelerator Design and Hardware Security.

During my Ph.D., I have been working on Hardware implementation and acceleration of Post Quantum Cryptographic Algorithms, and on design and implementation of Physically Unclonable Functions (PUF) for building secure protocols.

I completed my M.Tech in CSE from University of Kalyani, supervised by Dr. Anirban Mukhopadhyay and Dr. Sujoy Chatterjee. My B.E. was from Burdwan University.

I also worked as a JRF at NIT Durgapur on lightweight FPGA security architectures, supervised by Dr. Bibhash Sen.
